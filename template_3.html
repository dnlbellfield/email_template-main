Unlock Your Energy: 3 Simple Habits

Start with Sunlight

Expose yourself to natural light within 30 minutes of waking to regulate your circadian rhythm and boost alertness.

Hydrate Intelligently

Drink a glass of water immediately upon waking and continue to sip throughout the day for sustained energy.

Incorporate Gentle Movement

Even 5-10 minutes of stretching or light walking can significantly improve blood flow and mood.
